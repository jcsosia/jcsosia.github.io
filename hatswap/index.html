Swap into the app you need, when you need it.

HatSwap is a minimalist app launcher that switches your app choices based on your schedule, your context, and your current workflow. It stays local, lightweight, and easy to tune.

Play Store Feedback Privacy

What it does

HatSwap swaps apps based on the schedule you define, so the right tools are easier to reach when your day changes.

Designed for small, intentional workflows

The app keeps the experience focused on the task at hand, with local storage, scheduled switching, and a few guardrails when rules overlap.

Features

Everything HatSwap is built to support.

  • Schedule-based app switching
  • Custom hats for different contexts
  • Widget integration
  • Manual override with Hotswap
  • Priority-based conflict resolution
  • Local backup and restore
